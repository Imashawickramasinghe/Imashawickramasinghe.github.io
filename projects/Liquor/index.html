The problem
Lots of are like jacob !

When a busy group of family members or a group of friends get together to buy liquor for an occasion, it’s always a pain when deciding what liquor to buy, how much should they spend for them, how they going to split the cost and to find the nearest liquor store. People always have to use a messaging app or voice calls to discuss these things and its always annoying when a group collect cash from each other to pay for the bill.
Hypothesis
It is 21 century, why not have alcohol delivered to your doorstep? An app that helps users to search for different kinds of liquor, buy the liquor and get delivered to your doorstep, if there is a group of people create a chat within the app and discuss things, pay alone for the bill or to divide the bill among the group of friends and pay their share separately will make it easy to enjoy a party for busy people without doing things in the old fashion way.
The why ?
when people get too busy, they always search for the easiest way to get things done. While almost everything is been able to do by a mobile phone, there is no easy way to shop liquor in Srilanka. Though there are platforms to buy liquor online there is no platform specifically only for liquor shopping and there is no way to divide the total bill and pay separately for your own share of the bill. One person has to pay for the bill and after paying others has to pay him back. This is always messy path to take.
Business Opertunities
This is an app that that will make path for a new business opportunity. Though there are online platforms like keels and Ceylon spirits, there are no popular apps for alcohol shopping in Srilanka. And the bill splitting concept is new here. There is no platform to do planning, chatting, shopping, and get delivered in a one place. In 21 st century everyone likes to use an attractive app rather that browsing the internet for a web site.
